Elastic Faceted Search
01. Improved search relevance

Accelerate conversion rate.

Our feature-rich platform unlocks more sales potential with a simple, concise faceted search. This advanced search adds appropriate product filters that help your shoppers to find products faster and easier. Valuable attributes such as category, sizes, type, color, rating and brand are exposed making it easier for customers to sort, filter and shop.

02. Effective catalog browsing

Search is the primary purchase path.

Customize the search interface according to the shopper’s browsing behavior. Collaborative filtering and personalization offered by elastic faceted search ensure better product catalog viewing, often converting the upper funnel visitors into potential customers.

03. Relevant product category and filters

Earn the loyalty of your customers.

Navicommerce ensures excellent browsing experience with an elastic faceted search that will maximize traffic to your website and give your customers a significant reason to come back. We add an extensive list of facets or product filters and data structures that are relevant to the product you sell making it easier for shoppers to find exactly what they are looking for. You can choose to place the faceted search vertically or horizontally based on the number of facets you add.

04. Easy to maintain, seamless implementation

Personalize every browsing experience.

We offer unlimited customizations and keep the implementation process. The filters can be configured easily and controlled from a centralized location enabling you to add, delete, hide filters, add a search bar, tooltips or edit filter titles.

05. Smart dashboard with filter statistics

Identify buying patterns & behavior.

Intelligent data–driven insights derived from scaling the search history, help you to recognize popular products, categories, patterns and trends. Following these accurate insights, you can modify your stock and push the conversion rate.
